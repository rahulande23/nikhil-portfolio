About me

STORY
TELLER

I'm Nikhil Thatipamula — a senior video editor and colorist with over 8 years shaping raw footage into experiences audiences can't forget. I've worked with global brands, indie filmmakers, and award-winning directors across three continents.

My philosophy is simple: every cut should be invisible when it serves the story and unforgettable when it defines a moment. I work across DaVinci Resolve, Premiere Pro, and After Effects, bringing a colorist's eye to every project.
